About me

Operator with durability, speed, and control

I’ve spent 15 years leading operations across complex portfolios and high-volume environments where the margin for error is low and the cost of drift is real. My work lives at the intersection of execution and controls: keeping teams moving fast while ensuring decisions remain auditable, vendors remain accountable, and financial outcomes are measurable.

I’m typically brought in when the operation is growing, changing, or under pressure, and the existing way of working no longer scales. The pattern is consistent: clarify ownership, standardize inputs and escalation paths, and build lightweight governance that survives peak load. The goal isn’t paperwork. The goal is a system that produces reliable outcomes without heroics, and gives leadership clean visibility into performance, risk, and priorities.

I’m known as a trusted advisor to ownership and senior stakeholders because I can translate operational reality into decision-ready plans, and then drive disciplined follow-through with teams and vendors. If you want an operator who produces stability, throughput, and predictable results, that’s the lane I run in.
